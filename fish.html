@KHATUN MAMMADOVA

"Xatun Memmedova was born on May 25, 1945, in the village of Kele in Dagestan. In 1965, she married her partner Qasim Memmedov . Qasim was a Russian language and literature teacher. In 1975, as a family, they moved to the town of Zaqatala. In that town, Xatun worked as a baker in a bakery. Currently, Xatun lives in the town of Zaqatala with her eldest son, Bunyamin."
