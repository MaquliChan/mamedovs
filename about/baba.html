@QASIM MAMMADOV

"Qasim Memmedov was a teacher of Russian language and literature. He was born on September 25, 1940 in Kelal village. In 1966, he married Xatun Memmedova. They lived in Kelal until 1973, and then they moved as a family to Zaqatala. In that year, he started working at Zaqatala Secondary School. Qasim and Xatun had 5 children: Bunyamin, Minay, Susen, Suray, and Isa. Sadly, on August 29, 2014, Qasim passed away."
