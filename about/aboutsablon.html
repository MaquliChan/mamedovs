@RAMAZAN MAMMADOV

Ramazan Mamedov is a 5th-year student at Azerbaijan Medical University's dental faculty. He was born on January 2, 2002, in Zaqatala, Azerbaijan. In 2008, he started attending Nizami Huseynov Secondary School No. 2 in Zaqatala. He graduated from the same school in 2019 and was accepted into the dental faculty at Azerbaijan Medical University. Ramazan is currently a 5th-year student at AMU.
